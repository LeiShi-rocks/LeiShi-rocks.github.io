Research

Research Interest

My current research interest includes:

  • High dimensional statistics: estimation, model selection and inference for static or dynamic high dimensional structures, including sparse PCA/CCA, robust low rank matrix recovery, change point detection in high dimensional regression, etc.

  • Causal inference: design and analysis of randomized experiments.

  • Learning theory: statistical learning/machine learning, reinforcement learning, deep learning, etc.

Publications

  • Shi, L, Zou, C. “Noisy Low Rank Matrix Completion Under General Bases”. Stat. 2020;e303.

Working projects

  • Rank Matrix LASSO: a tuning-free robust scheme for high-dimensional low-rank matrix estimation

  • Hierarchical factorial model selection under the finite-population framework

  • Large-Q-small-Nz: causal inference in factorial experiments with many factors and limited replications

  • Subgroup analysis with confidence distribution in increasing dimensions
